Title Company

RESPA Section 9 of the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act prohibits Sellers from conditioning their home sale on the use of a specific title insurance company and in fact violators can be subject to penalties with the most typical being fined up to three times the amount of the insurance fee.

HIT lists Title companies if and only if they are willing to publish their fee sheet on the site. HIT wants to disclose to you all fees throughout the process and title closing fees are on the list.

If you have a single favorite Title Company and closer, please fill out the following form, HIT will default all MLS and Non MLS transactions

As investors there are times when using the Seller's title company makes better sense than taking the deal to your favorite closer. HIT recommends the flowing - please check the appropriate box.

HIT will cascade other Title Companies willing to publish fees at this part of the contract so our Subscribers are kept up to date on fees being charged by competitive Title Insurance companies; HIT will default to your selected Title Company in the normal course of contract generation
